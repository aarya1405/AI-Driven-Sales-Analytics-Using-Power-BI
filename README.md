# AI-Driven-Sales-Analytics-Using-Power-BI

(Global Superstore Dataset)

**Project Overview**

This project delivers an AI-driven sales analytics dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI, leveraging the Global Superstore dataset to analyze business performance, identify sales drivers, uncover risk factors, and support strategic decision-making.

The solution combines traditional BI techniques with Power BI’s AI visuals to move beyond descriptive reporting into diagnostic and insight-driven analytics.

**Objectives**

- Provide an executive-level overview of sales and profitability

- Identify key drivers and risk factors impacting sales performance

- Analyze sales and profit trends over time

- Evaluate the impact of discounting across product categories

- Perform regional, product, and segment-level diagnostic analysis

- Demonstrate AI-assisted analytics and business storytelling

**Dataset Details**

- Dataset Name: Global Superstore

- Domain: Retail / Sales Analytics

- Granularity: Order-level transactional data

- Time Span: Multi-year historical data

- Geographic Coverage: Global (multiple regions and markets)

**Key Fields Used
**
- Order Date

- Region, Market, Country

- Product Category & Sub-Category

- Customer Segment

- Ship Mode

- Sales, Profit, Quantity, Discount

A dedicated Date table was created to enable accurate time-based analysis and consistent filtering across visuals.
**
Dashboard Structure & Analysis
1. Executive Overview – AI Narrative**

**Purpose:**
High-level snapshot of overall business performance for decision-makers.

**Key Visuals:**

- KPI Cards: Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Quantity, Average Discount

- Monthly Sales Trend

- Monthly Profit Trend

-  Narrative (AI visual)

**Insights:**

- Consistent growth in sales and profit

- Automatically generated insights using Smart Narrative to summarize performance trends

2️. Sales Drivers & Risk Factors (AI Analysis)

**Purpose:**
Identify factors influencing sales increases and declines.

**Key Visuals:**

- Key Influencers (AI visual)

- Category-wise contribution to total sales

- Interactive filters (Year, Region)

**Insights:**

- Sales declines linked to specific shipping modes, regions, and customer segments

- Highlights operational inefficiencies and cost–benefit misalignment

**3. Trend Insights & Performance Signals**
**
Purpose:**
Evaluate long-term growth patterns and pricing effectiveness.

**Key Visuals:**

- Annual Sales & Profit Growth (combo chart)

- Discount vs Sales impact (scatter chart)

- Category-wise Sales & Profit contribution

**Insights:**

- Steady year-over-year growth

- Higher discounts do not always correlate with higher sales

- Technology category leads in revenue and profitability

**4. Regional & Product Performance Deep-Dive**

**Purpose:**
Support root-cause and diagnostic analysis.

**Key Visuals:**

- KPI Matrix (Sales, Profit, Average Discount by Region)

- Decomposition Tree (AI visual)

- Analytical summary text

**Insights:
**
- Clear regional and category-level performance variations

- Interactive drill-down to identify high-impact region–category–segment combinations

**AI Features Used in Power BI**

- Smart Narrative: Auto-generated written insights

- Key Influencers: Identifies drivers behind sales changes

- Decomposition Tree: AI-assisted root-cause analysis

These features transform the dashboard into a decision-support system rather than a static report.

**Tools & Technologies**

- Microsoft Power BI Desktop

- DAX for KPIs and measures

- Power Query for data transformation

- AI Visuals (Smart Narrative, Key Influencers, Decomposition Tree)
